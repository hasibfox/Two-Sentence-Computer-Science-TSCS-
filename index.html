About Me

Hey! I'm Mohammad Hasib, a freshman at Wesleyan University who is trying to get a grasp at the field of computer science. I built this website for two main reasons: to reflect on my learnings and to simplify concepts down into two sentencese. As with any subject, the best way to internalize what one learn is by teaching it to others and I believe that through Two Sentence CS, I can work on mastering course content myself while also providing helpful reminders to others. I strongly encourage you to scroll down to get a better idea of who I am, some of my (prospective) projects, and my "No-Regret" outlook!
